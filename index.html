About Publications

My name is Marco Favorito. Currently, I work as a researcher in the Applied Research Team of the Bank of Italy.

I obtained my PhD in 2022 from Sapienza University of Rome (DIAG), where I worked in the research group WhiteMech, under the supervision of prof. Giuseppe De Giacomo.

Previously, I was a full-time Machine Learning Engineer at Fetch.ai. I am one of the main contributors of the AEA framework (original repo, fork), which empowers developers to build decentralized autonomous software that trades on blockchains and perform other useful tasks for their owner.

I have got both a MSc. and BSc. in Engineering in Computer Science at Sapienza University, in 2016 and 2018 respectively.

For the list of my publications, please look at my Google Scholar and DBLP profiles, or this page.
